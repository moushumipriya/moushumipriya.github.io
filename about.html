About Me

Who Am I?

I am Mst.Moushumi Khatun, a Published AI Engineer, Research Assistant, and aspiring PhD researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Trustworthy AI, Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Multi-Agent Systems, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Deep Learning. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT). My research focuses on building safe, ethical, intelligent, and scalable AI systems capable of solving real-world challenges in healthcare, autonomous reasoning, predictive analytics, and decision-support environments.

Professional Experience

Currently working as a JR. AI Engineer at Save The Children International, where I develop RLHF pipelines, reward models, and ethical AI systems for child safeguarding applications. I am also working as a Research Assistant at Advanced Machine Intelligence Research Lab, contributing to advanced deep learning systems, computer vision architectures, multi-modal learning frameworks, and autonomous agent systems. Previously, I worked as a Senior Instructor at BritSync UK and as a Junior Data Scientist, where I mentored students and developed AI/ML projects and learning materials.

Research Interests

My research interests include: • Trustworthy AI • Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) • Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning • LLM Alignment • Agentic AI Systems • NLP & Generative AI • Federated Learning • Deep Learning Architectures • AI Safety & Ethical AI • Computer Vision • Sequential Decision Systems • Autonomous Scientific Ideation Systems I am particularly interested in designing safe collaborative AI systems that can reason, communicate, and make intelligent decisions in decentralized environments.

Publications & Research

My research work has been published in IEEE, ACM, Springer, and internationally recognized conferences. I also have an accepted manuscript in PLOS ONE. My publication domains include: • Lane Segmentation in Autonomous Vehicles • Human Activity Recognition • Federated Learning for Cybersecurity • Deep Learning in Agriculture • Sentiment-Aware Team Forecasting • AI Safety Systems • Multi-Agent Learning Architectures

Career Goal

My long-term goal is to pursue a fully funded PhD in Artificial Intelligence, focusing on Trustworthy AI, RLHF, Agentic AI, and decentralized intelligent systems. I aspire to contribute to cutting-edge AI research that creates positive social impact globally through ethical, safe, and collaborative intelligent systems.
